## Overview

Get started with Compitio by signing up for early access and preparing for the platform launch. Compitio provides innovative tools for your business needs. Follow these steps to register, access the dashboard, complete the initial setup, and navigate the interface.

<Callout kind="info">
  Compitio is launching soon. Sign up for early access to receive updates and beta invitations via email.
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for registration
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- Access to `https://dashboard.example.com` (available post-launch)

<Callout kind="tip">
  Join the waitlist now to be among the first users when Compitio goes live.
</Callout>

## Sign Up for Early Access

Use the following steps to register for early access.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Website" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the Compitio homepage at `https://compitio.com` (coming soon).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Fill out the early access form with your name, email, and company details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="check-circle">
    Log in and update your profile with preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Accessing the Dashboard

Once approved for early access:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Open `https://dashboard.example.com` and sign in with your credentials.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download the beta app from the provided link in your welcome email and log in.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Initial Setup Wizard

The setup wizard guides you through first-time configuration.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Account" icon="link">
    Authorize Compitio to access your linked services.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set API Key" icon="key">
    Generate and save your API key for integrations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Dashboard" icon="settings">
    Select your preferred theme and modules.
  </Step>
</Steps>

```javascript
// Example: Save your API key securely
const API_KEY = 'your-api-key-here';
localStorage.setItem('compitio_api_key', API_KEY);
console.log('API key saved for dashboard access.');
```
