## Overview

Stay informed about Compitio's launch by joining the newsletter.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Compitio.com">
    ````
    Visit https://compitio.com (site may display 'Coming Soon')
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the signup form">
    Look for the newsletter or updates section on the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Input your email address and click "Subscribe" or "Notify Me".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm subscription">
    Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the link.
  </Step>
</Steps>
